Assignment 6, Question 1
  
(a) Recursive definition of palindrome:
  A palindrome is of the form XAX where X is a single character, and A is a palindrome.  Empty strings, and single characters are palindromes.
  
  
(b) Recursive definition of SubList equals:
  Two SubLists are equal if their root objects are equal, and if their remainder sublists are equal recursively.  Two empty/null SubLists are equal.
